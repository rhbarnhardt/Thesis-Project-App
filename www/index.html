In Their Worlds

Stories of Mental Illness,

told by those affected by it.

Life and Law

Chapter One

Mental illness elicits many different reactions from society, many of which are negative. In the US, one in four adults live with mental illness, yet crippling stigma surrounds those living with a diagnosis. Jack has dedicated his professional life to breaking down that stigma by using his own diagnosis.

Two People

Chapter Two

As a child, Jane didn’t understand why her mother seemed to be two different people. There was the loving mother, and the harshly critical woman she came to fear.It wasn’t until she was an adult that Jane realized that addiction was the cause for this split. In this chapter, Jane shares her story of coming to terms with addiction, and how she has found reconciliation.

Strength in Community

Chapter Three

Jack was serving in the Navy in Vietnam when his sister was diagnosed with Schizophrenia. For a time, he felt guilty that he wasn’t there in the early days. Today, Jack is inspired by the strength of his sister, and sees the positive affect that support networks have on the diagnosed and their families.
